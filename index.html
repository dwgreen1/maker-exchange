Frequently Asked Questions

Local artists, makers, crafters, reformed hoarders, creative individuals, or anyone who wants to clean out old supplies and possibly find some new ones.

New, like-new and gently used arts, craft and maker supplies. A good rule of thumb: if I can't use it right away (i.e., it needs a lot of TLC to get it to a workable state) don't donate it. Here are some possible items that you might want to donate:

Painting

  • Paint brushes
  • Acrylic paints
  • Oil paints
  • Watercolors
  • Stencils
  • Gel media
  • Canvas

Drawing

  • Pencils
  • Pens
  • Markers
  • Charcoal
  • Erasers
  • Sketch books

Ceramics

  • Ribs
  • Carving tools
  • Molds

Scrapbooking

  • Paper
  • Stamps
  • Washi tape
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • Tape

Jewelry making

  • Beads
  • Findings

Fiber Art

  • Fabric
  • Yarn
  • Thread
  • Needles
  • Buttons
  • Zippers

Baking

  • Pans
  • Cookie cutters
  • Frosting tips
  • Molds (Nothing edible!)

Hobby Electronics

  • Microcontrollers

Gadgets, Tools, Small Equipment

  • Multimeters

Library pop-ups are a free shopping experience

Franklinton Friday pop-ups will have very reasonable prices and lots of fill-a-bag specials

We are looking to bring more art supply options into the Franklinton area, while also giving back to the greater Columbus community. We also hate to see good supplies go to waste, so we're collecting from the folks who no longer need them and redistributing them to folks who have the ideas and time to make something awesome.

For reference, here are some other folks doing this type of thing:

  • Craft Raccoon
  • Upcycle Parts Shop
  • Sister Trillium<
  • Scrap It Up
  • Pittsburgh Center for Creative Reuse
  • Rewind Reuse Center
  • Thistlebit Creative Reuse
  • Austin Creative Reuse

If you want to donate supplies but can’t make the listed times, reach out and let us know when you're available, and we can figure something out.

If you want to shop items but can’t make that time, keep an eye on this page for future events. Or add our Google calendar.
