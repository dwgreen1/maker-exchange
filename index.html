Nothing! This is an experiment to see how much is out there and how this could work.

There are creative recycle and upcycle shops all around, but I haven't found one in Columbus yet (that might be changing soon because of the awesome, creative people in this city). I hate to see good supplies go to waste, so I want to start collecting from the folks who no longer need them and redistributing them to folks who have the ideas and time to make something awesome. If things go well, maybe someday this can turn into something bigger.

For reference, here are some other folks doing this type of thing:

  • Upcycle Parts Shop
  • Sister Trillium<
  • Scrap It Up
  • Pittsburgh Center for Creative Reuse
  • Rewind Reuse Center
  • Thistlebit Creative Reuse
  • Austin Creative Reuse
